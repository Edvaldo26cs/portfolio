About me

I am passionate about web development, with solid training in technologies such as HTML, CSS3, JavaScript, Bootstrap, Vue.js, and PHP (Laravel), as well as experience building responsive websites. Although I don't have formal professional experience yet, I dedicate myself to learning and developing personal projects that reflect my drive to turn ideas into functional digital experiences.

Throughout my learning journey, I've created several applications and websites that have strengthened my skills in front-end and back-end development, as well as working with SQL Server Management Studio and creating tables in ServiceNow.

In addition to development, I have a strong focus on Quality Assurance (QA) testing, particularly test automation using tools like Selenium, Cypress, and Ruby. I believe that high-quality code and reliable functionality are essential, and I continuously seek to improve my testing and development practices.

I’m a strong believer in clean, functional, and responsive design that not only meets user needs but also exceeds expectations. I'm excited to collaborate on challenging projects and contribute my creativity, commitment, and enthusiasm to the success of any team.
